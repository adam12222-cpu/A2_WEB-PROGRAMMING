Minecraft Structures

Different types of structures in Minecraft

Villages

villages can spawn in different biomes. Villages have villages that trades items to the player/s, they also have farmlands, animal pens and an iron golem, the protector of the village.

Pillager outpost

Tall watchtowers occupied by hostile Illagers that patrol the surrounding area.

Woodland Mansion

Huge, rare forest mansions filled with Evokers, Vindicators, and hidden rooms.

Desert Pyramids

Sandstone temples hiding loot chambers protected by a TNT trap.

Jungle Temple

Mossy cobblestone temples featuring lever puzzles and arrow traps.

Igloo

Snowy huts that sometimes conceal a hidden basement laboratory.

Ancient City

Vast underground ruins in the Deep Dark, guarded by the powerful Warden.

Trial Chambers

Large, procedurally generated combat structures made of copper and tuff.

Stronghold

Underground labyrinths that contain the End Portal to reach the End dimension.

Abandoned Mineshaft

Wooden-supported tunnels filled with rails, cobwebs, and scattered loot.

Ocean Monument

Enormous prismarine structures inhabited by dangerous Guardians.

Ocean Ruins

Underwater remains of ancient villages made of stone or sandstone.

Shipwreck

Sunken or beached ships that often contain treasure-filled chests.

Trail Ruins

Buried structures made of terracotta and mud bricks, rich in archaeology finds.

Dungeon

Small cobblestone rooms with a monster spawner and loot chests.

Ruined Portal

Broken Nether portals scattered across the world, often with useful loot nearby.

Nether Fortress

Large, dark brick structures in the Nether inhabited by Blazes, Wither Skeletons, and other hostile mobs.

End Ship

Floating structures found in End Cities that contain valuable loot and an Elytra.

Did you Know?

  • Woodland Mansions are so rare that you might need to travel thousands of blocks before finding one?
  • Villages can spawn in different biomes, but the rarest village is a village that spawns in either a swamp biome or mangrove.
  • After finding a small room under an Igloo, you can take the golden apple and the potion of weakness to cure the zombified villager.
