What is Minecraft?

Minecraft is a game where you can let your imagination run free and build anything using blocks. You can also explore your world, gather resources, and fight monsters.

Theres usually two game modes players chose:

  • ⚔Survival Mode, where you need to eat, build a base, and fend off mobs like zombies and skeletons.
  • ⚒Creative Mode, where have flight, and have access to every block the game has to offer to build whatever creative ideas you might have

Minecraft is also one of the best-selling games ever, with over 300 million copies sold worldwide.

Fun facts🔮

  • 300+ Mil copies sold💰
  • 100+ Mil players every month🎮
  • over 830+ different blocks to build with!

Three Dimensions

  • Overworld: Where you start off your journey. The Main world
  • Nether: Hell-like dimension. Where you will need to gather resources to get into the last world.
  • The End: The Last dimension there is. This is also where you will meet the ender dragon.
